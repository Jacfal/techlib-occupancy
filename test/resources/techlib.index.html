Trial access to Public Library Video Online

28. 3.– We have launched a trial to Public Library Video Online, produced by Alexander Street, a division of ProQuest that provides unlimited access to nearly 50,000 streaming videos. A trial to ProQuest Dissertations & Theses Global was also launched, covering the full range of dissertations. Both trials will run until April 30, 2022, and are linked in the list of electronic resources.
